<p><strong>Highlights of 2024:</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Achieved 'Gold' for Company Award (2023)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Staff Sergeants See Xin Hui, Chee Xin Min Josephine,</p>
<p>Renae Tay Rui Ni and Nueng Ang Zhi Min received the Pioneer Brigader Brooch
Award in 2024, the highest award attained in the Girls Brigade.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Lance Corporal Au Yong Qiu Yun represented in the National Day Parade
2024 as part of the marching Contingent</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 2 and 3 girls participated in Non-Commissioned Officers' Training
Camp(NCOTC), a National Camp organised by the GB Singapore</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>5 groups of GB girls participated in the Dr Low Guat Tin (LGT) Challenge
2024, to the theme “Better together, for a better tomorrow</p>
</li>
</ul>
