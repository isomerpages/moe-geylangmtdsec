<p><strong>Highlights of 2024:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Achieved a Distinction for Unit Overall Proficiency Award (2023).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Two cadets were awarded the highest award for a NPCC cadet, that is, SPF-NPCC
Badge. They are Daniel Pe Win (P45) and Tan Qi Hoong (P44).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 1 cadets participated in a Bouldering course.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 2 cadets participated in one star Kayaking course.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 3 &amp; 4 cadets represented the unit in a Shooting competition organized
by NPCC Hq.</p>
</li>
</ul>
