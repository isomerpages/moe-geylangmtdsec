<p>Dear Parents/ Guardian and our newly posted Secondary 1 students,</p>
<p></p>
<p>Greetings from Geylang Methodist School (Secondary) and a very warm welcome
to you and your child to our GMS(S) family.</p>
<p>The Secondary One Registration process will be conducted online. You and
your child are&nbsp;<strong>not required</strong>&nbsp;to report in person
on Registration Day. To help you and your child prepare for the new school
year, key information will be sent via Parents Gateway (PG).&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Online Registration</u>
</p>
<p>The online registration will open on Registration Day, <strong>19 December 2025, Friday, 9am</strong>.</p>
<p>To e-register your child, go to <a href="https://form.gov.sg/693b0d08c85dd68664744397" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Geylang Methodist School (Secondary) 2026 Secondary 1 e-Registration | FormSG</a> .</p>
<p>It is compulsory to complete all the fields in the online registration</p>
<p><strong>Please complete the</strong>  <strong>online registration latest by 23 December 2025, Tuesday, 12pm</strong>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Information on Parents Gateway (PG)</u>
</p>
<p>To help you and your child prepare for the new school year, key information
will be sent via Parents Gateway (PG). We have also included the following
documents for your easy reference to all the information that you need.</p>
<p>-&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; School General Information</p>
<p>-&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Checklist for Parents on Secondary
1 Matters 2026</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
<p><u>Subject(s) at a More Demanding Level, Higher Mother Tongue Language and /or 3rd Language</u>
</p>
<p>If your child is eligible to take subject(s) at a More Demanding Level,
Higher Mother Tongue Language and /or 3rd Language, separate offer letter(s)
will be sent to you via PG.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Class and House Allocation</u>
</p>
<p>The Class and House allocations for your child/ward will be <strong>sent to you via PG when available.</strong> You
can then proceed to buy your child’s/ward’s PE attire according to the
house colour.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Archer (Red),</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Lau (Green),</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Means (Yellow) and</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Mitchell (Blue).</p>
<p></p>
<p>There are 3 modes for purchase of school uniform and PE attire from our
uniform vendor, Bibi &amp; Baba.</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Online purchase with option for delivery or
self-collection at GM(S). Click <a href="https://www.schooluniforms.sg/geylang-methodist-secondary-school" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Walk-in purchase at Bibi &amp; Baba’s retail
store</p>
<p>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Walk-in purchase in GMS(S)</p>
<p>Please refer to the GMS(S) Sale of School Uniform PDF more details for
walk-in purchase.</p>
<p>Class and House allocation is also displayed on the noticeboard outside
the General Office until the end of Term 1 Week 1.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Application for MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS)</u>
</p>
<p>The Ministry of Education provides financial assistance to Singaporean
students from our Government and Government-Aided Schools who need financial
assistance for their studies.</p>
<p>To submit the e-FAS form, please click <a href="https://form.gov.sg/68b7e92465cd36be287889c6" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>Click <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> for
more information on the MOE Financial Assistant Scheme and the Financial
assistant eligibility checker.</p>
<p>Please approach our staff in the General Office before you purchase the
textbooks and uniform.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Appeal for Transfer<br></u><strong><br>Serious Medical Conditions:</strong>
<br>Students who wish to appeal for <strong>transfer to other schools</strong> due
to medical reasons, please submit your appeal to our school’s General Office&nbsp;<strong>by</strong>  <strong>22 Dec 2025, 12.00 pm.</strong> You
are required to bring the supporting medical documents.
<br>&nbsp;
<br><strong>Non-Medical Reasons</strong>:
<br>Students who wish to appeal for <strong>transfer to other schools</strong> due
to non-medical reasons, please submit the appeal&nbsp;<strong>directly</strong>&nbsp;<strong>to the preferred school.</strong>
</p>
<p>For more MOE information on Appeal for transfer, please click <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/secondary/s1-posting/results/appeal-for-school-transfer" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>For appeal to <strong>transfer into GMS(S)</strong> other than medical reason,
students must <strong>first meet our school’s cut off point</strong> (COP).</p>
<p>For submission of appeal to transfer into GMS(S), kindly submit via FormSG
by clicking <a href="https://form.gov.sg/6921163c1b0657c649754d4f" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.
Please submit <strong>by 24 Dec 2025, 12.00pm</strong>.</p>
<p></p>
